Switch from cubic (a^3 decay rate) to quartic (a^4 decay rate).
The quartic gives a 1/t^(1/3) tail — even slower than cubic's 1/sqrt(t).
With lambda_fact=0.008, impact=0: a(200) ≈ 0.44 vs cubic 0.35.

The only metric with room to improve is recall@0.5 (currently 0.483
in exp_0004, ceiling 0.498). The quartic keeps all items at higher
activation, so more items stay above 0.5.

However, correlation will likely drop further since there's even less
activation spread. The bet: recall@0.5 gain > correlation loss.
