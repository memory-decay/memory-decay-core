To recover recall and achieve high precision lift under the new multiplicative gate, this experiment introduces a sigmoid-gated floor based on a combined importance score (impact and stability). Memories with importance > 0.3 are protected by a high floor (0.5), maintaining recall. Distractors (< 0.3) lose their floor and experience accelerated decay (prune_factor = 2.5) to clear them from similarity rankings, maximizing precision lift.
