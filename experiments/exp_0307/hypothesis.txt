Bi-exponential decay: fast (hippocampal) + slow (neocortical) components.

Low-importance memories dominated by fast component (lambda=0.06) → rapid
death. High-importance memories dominated by slow component (lambda=0.008)
→ long survival. Mixing weight = importance*1.5 capped at 1.0.

This creates natural separation: important memories have fat tails while
unimportant ones die quickly. Should improve both recall (important survive)
and precision_lift (unimportant pruned) simultaneously.
