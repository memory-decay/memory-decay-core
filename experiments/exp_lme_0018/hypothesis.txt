Hyperbolic decay instead of Jost power law.

Instead of: da/dt = -lambda * (a - floor)^jost_power
Use: da/dt = -lambda * (a - floor) / ((a - floor) + hyperbolic_k)

This hyperbolic form has different properties:
- At high excess: approaches linear decay like Jost with power=1
- At low excess: much slower decay than power law
- More gradual approach to floor

May produce smoother forgetting curves.
