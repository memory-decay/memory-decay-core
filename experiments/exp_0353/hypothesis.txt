Combine stability_decay=0.003 (precision_lift benefit from exp_0351) with stability_weight=1.0→0.8.

Exp_0351 showed stability_decay=0.003 gives first positive precision_lift (0.0024) but
recall dropped from 0.402 to 0.382. Root cause: slower stability decay keeps weak
memories alive longer, crowding top-k.

Solution: reduce stability_weight from 1.0 to 0.8, which gives stability 20% less
influence on both the importance floor signal AND the retention formula. This directly
reduces the "weak memories staying alive" problem while preserving the precision_lift
benefit from slower stability fade.

Hypothesis: stability_weight=0.8 + stability_decay=0.003 gives precision_lift without
the recall penalty.

Rejected: exp_0352 (stability_decay=0.003 + lambda_fact=0.0095) still gave recall=0.381.
