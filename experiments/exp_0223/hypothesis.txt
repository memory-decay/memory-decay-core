Test memories never get direct reactivation — only associative reinforcement from
connected train memories. Default reinforcement_gain_assoc=0.05 is too low to create
meaningful stability differentiation among test memories. Increasing to 0.15 should
make test memories with many associations (likely more semantically central, more
likely to appear in similarity results) have higher stability → slower decay → higher
activation at tick 200 → improved activation-recall correlation.
Also: higher stability_weight pushes the stability effect further.
