Use different mathematical forms for facts vs episodes:
- Facts: cubic decay (a^3 rate, lambda=0.010) — very slow tail, nearly
  all facts survive at tick 200 regardless of impact.
- Episodes: quadratic decay (a^2 rate, lambda=0.035) — faster, so
  low-impact episodes fall below threshold while high-impact survive.

This creates a natural two-population structure: facts cluster high,
low-impact episodes die. The activation-recall correlation should improve
because the surviving population (facts + high-impact episodes) has
systematically higher activation than the dying population. Expected:
recall_mean ~0.42-0.46, correlation ~0.25-0.35, overall > 0.40.
