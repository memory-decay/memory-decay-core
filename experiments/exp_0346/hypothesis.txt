Three targeted micro-adjustments from best (exp_0315):

1. sigmoid_k 20 → 25: steeper importance discrimination curve. The best model's k=20
   already gives a sharp transition, but k=25 would sharpen it further — making
   high-importance memories get meaningfully higher floors than mid-importance ones.

2. lambda_fact 0.008 → 0.007: slightly slower fact decay. Facts are the bulk of
   test memories; preserving one more fact at tick 200 could shift recall from ~0.40.

3. assoc_boost 0 → 0.2: very conservative spreading activation. The assoc_boost range
   0.1-0.5 showed small improvements in exp_0333-0336 without destabilizing CV.
   This is below that range, maximally conservative.

Rejected: sigmoid_mid shift (0.25→0.28) collapsed MRR. Log decay collapsed recall.
Activation-boosted floor hurt both retrieval and plausibility.
