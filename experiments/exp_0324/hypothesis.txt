Hyperbolic decay with excess-dependent acceleration. Instead of Jost's
excess^power subtraction, use reciprocal form: excess/(1+rate*accel).
Hyperbolic decay has natural fat tails (O(1/t) convergence) which the
mathematician recommended as theoretically superior.

The acceleration factor (1 + 3*excess) makes high-activation memories
decay faster initially but preserve differentiation near floor.
