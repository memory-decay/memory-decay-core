Use random reactivation policy and higher assoc_boost to improve recall.

Previous experiments showed recall stuck at 0.62 because test memories are never directly reactivated - only train memories get scheduled reactivation. Test memories depend entirely on associative spreading.

This experiment:
1. Use random reactivation policy (reactivates ANY memory including test memories)
2. Increase assoc_boost to 0.5 (stronger spreading activation)
3. Slightly higher floor_max (0.55) for more baseline protection

The random policy means test memories can be directly reactivated, which should help maintain their activation over time.
