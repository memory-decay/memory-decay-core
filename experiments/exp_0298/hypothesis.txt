In exp_0297, setting sigmoid_mid to 0.3 accidentally penalized memories with impact 0.5 (which evaluate to exactly 0.3 importance initially). This caused recall to drop too much (0.13). By lowering sigmoid_mid to 0.22, memories with impact 0.4 and 0.5 will now be safely protected by the floor, while impact 0.3 and below will be heavily pruned (prune_factor=3.0). This should yield a better balance of high recall for important nodes and strong precision lift from pruning distractors.
