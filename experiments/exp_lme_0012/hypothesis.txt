Change stability from importance averaging to floor multiplier.

Previously: stability and impact were averaged into `importance = (impact*α + stability*ρ)/(α+ρ)`, then passed through sigmoid.

Now: Impact determines base sigmoid floor, stability acts as multiplicative boost:
  floor = floor_max * sigmoid(k * (impact - mid)) * (1 + stability_weight * stability)

This separates the two mechanisms:
- Impact → base floor threshold (sharp discrimination)
- Stability → floor multiplier (additional protection for reinforced memories)

This should better utilize stability - a highly reinforced memory can reach higher floor even if its initial impact was modest.
